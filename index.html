Premier League

September 27,2021 by Devinn Weller

The English Premier League or the EPL is the highest competitive football league in the United Kingdom. The league was established in the year 1992. The competition consists of 20 football teams hailing from either England or Wales. All teams compete in a 38 game season in which they play each team twice, once at their home ground and the other away. At the end of the season, the teams that finish in the 18th to 20th positions are all relegated or demoted to the Championship; the league below the Premier League.

Additionally the teams finishing in the 1st to 7th position qualify for three separate European competitions. The teams from 1st to 4th qualify for the highest European football competition, the UEFA Champions League. The teams finishing in 5th and 6th (the 6th club is included if the winner of the FA Cup finished above them in the league) qualify for the Europa League. Finally the team finishing in 7th qualifies for the newest European competition, the Europa Conference League. The following are the teams that are currently in the competition: Arsenal, Aston Villa, Brentford, Brighton and Hove Albion, Burnley, Chelsea, Crystal Palace, Everton, Leeds United, Leicester City, Liverpool, Manchester United, Manchester City, Newcastle United, Norwich City, Southampton, Tottenham Hotspur, Watford, West Ham United, Wolverhampton Wanderers.

Winners

September 27,2021 by Devinn Weller

Since its inception the Premier League has had 28 seasons with only 7 different teams claiming the title of English Premier League Champions. The first team to win the legaue was Blackburn Rovers, doing so in the league's inaugural season. The current champions are Manchester City. The most successful of the 7 teams is Manchester United, who has won the Premier League a grand total of 13 times.

The following is a list of the various winners and their total amount of championships:

  • Arsenal- 3
  • Blackburn Rovers- 1
  • Chelsea- 5
  • Manchester United- 13
  • Manchester City- 5
  • Leicester City- 1
  • Liverpool- 1
